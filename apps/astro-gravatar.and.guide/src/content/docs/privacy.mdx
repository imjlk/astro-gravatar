# Privacy

astro-gravatar.and.guide is a documentation and package marketing site. We aim to keep the site usable without forcing analytics on visitors.

## What we collect

When analytics is enabled, we use Google Analytics to measure broad site usage such as:

- page views
- top documentation paths
- referral and campaign traffic
- device and browser trends

We do not load Google Analytics until you explicitly accept analytics from the consent banner.

## How consent works

- If `PUBLIC_GA_MEASUREMENT_ID` is not configured, analytics stays fully disabled.
- If it is configured, the site asks for analytics consent on first visit.
- Selecting **Accept analytics** loads Google Analytics and stores your choice locally in your browser.
- Selecting **Decline** keeps analytics disabled and stores that preference locally.
- You can reopen the consent prompt at any time from the **Privacy settings** button in the site footer.

## Managing your preference

Use the **Privacy settings** control in the footer to change your choice. If you decline after previously accepting, future analytics collection from this site is disabled from that point onward.
